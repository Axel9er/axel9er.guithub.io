ShowInsight is a charming and sensual yet modern interpretation of a cabaret show, featuring world-class dancers, musicians, vocalists, and DJs in an atmosphere of pure gold. The night evolves into a club from 12:30am onwards, featuring resident and guest DJs from around the world.
Club operation begins after the closing of the show (approximately 12:30am). Tables are transferred to our specially designed area in front of and adjacent to the DJ booth. Our resident DJs perform nightly, with scheduled guest DJs throughout the season.
ShowInsight welcomes chic evening style with an elegant finish. Guests are encouraged to dress in a way that reflects the energy, sophistication, and atmosphere of the venue. Casual attire, sportswear, and overly informal outfits are not permitted. Admission remains at the discretion of the door team and management.
We offer Bronze (from $2,500), Silver (from $6,500), and Gold (from $15,000) packages, as well as fully bespoke arrangements for those requiring something unique. All packages include a dedicated event manager and pre-event consultation. Contact our team for a personalised quote.
Simply submit a booking request through our Reservations page and our events team will reach out within 24 hours with a tailored proposal. There is no commitment until you are completely happy with the plan. We handle everything from performer selection to full production.
